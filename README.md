## 🧑‍💻 About Me

I'm a passionate **Backend & Full-Stack Developer** focused on building robust, scalable systems and clean, user-friendly interfaces. I care about shipping software that actually works.

- 🔭 Crafting efficient backend solutions with **Spring Boot** and **Go/Echo**
- 🎨 Building dynamic frontends with **Vue.js**, **Nuxt**, and **React**
- 🐳 Containerizing everything with **Docker** & **Podman**
- 🐧 Huge fan of **Linus Torvalds** and the Linux philosophy
- ⚡ Always looking for the cleanest solution to any problem
